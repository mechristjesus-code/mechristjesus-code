## Why YouTube for Person Research

Written profiles and Wikipedia entries provide facts, but YouTube reveals the person: how they think in real-time, how they handle pressure, their communication style, their genuine passions, and their unscripted opinions. Long-form interviews and podcast appearances are especially valuable because the subject speaks at length without heavy editing, often revealing insights they would never put in a press release or social media post.

## Video Discovery Strategy

Search in this priority order:

| Priority | Video type | Search query pattern | What it reveals |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Long-form interviews | `[person name] interview YouTube` | Thinking process, values, candid opinions |
| 2 | Podcast appearances | `[person name] podcast YouTube` | Most unscripted, deep-dive conversations |
| 3 | Keynote/conference talks | `[person name] keynote talk YouTube` | Professional vision, public persona |
| 4 | Panel discussions | `[person name] panel discussion YouTube` | How they interact with peers, debate style |
| 5 | Documentaries/profiles | `[person name] documentary profile YouTube` | Background, life story, context |
| 6 | Their own channel content | `[person name] YouTube channel` | Self-curated image, direct communication |
| 7 | Lecture/teaching content | `[person name] lecture class YouTube` | Expertise depth, teaching ability |
| 8 | Casual/personal vlogs | `[person name] vlog behind the scenes YouTube` | Personality, daily life, authenticity |
| 9 | Reaction/commentary about them | `[person name] analysis reaction YouTube` | Public perception, controversies |

**Search tips:**

- If the person is known by multiple names or aliases, search each variant.
- For non-English speakers, search in both English and their native language.
- Add context keywords to narrow results: `[person] on [topic]`, `[person] at [event]`.
- For recent activity, add the current year to queries.
- Search for specific known appearances: `[person] on [show/podcast name] YouTube`.

Run **2-3 search calls** with different query variants. Select **4-6 videos** spanning different contexts (formal interview, casual podcast, keynote, etc.) to build a multi-dimensional profile.

> **Note on Analysis Prompts**: See `prompt-templates.md` for specific high-density prompts for long-form interviews, podcast appearances, keynotes, and casual content.
